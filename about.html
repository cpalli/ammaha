About Ammaha

Ammaha is an end-to-end solutions company for sentient beings, currently working towards catering to humans. Our vision is to free the mind, and our mission is to pursue all the plausible ideas that result in structured solutions to materialize our vision.

Phase 0: Raising Capital

Free the Energy

We, Ammaha Energy, are the foundation of Ammaha’s vision. Our mission is to capture 21% of the energy market by investing in near-zero-operational cost energy solutions.

  1. Rooftop Solar & Wind Energy Solutions
  2. Large Scale Renewable Energy and Storage Solutions
  3. Sustainable E-Fuels (Green Hydrogen, Methanol and Ammonia)

Phase 1: Healthy Self and Surroundings

Free the Essentials

Ammaha Sustenance aims to address the basic needs and essentials of sentient beings through innovative and affordable solutions that facilitate a healthy self and healthy surroundings.

  1. Healthy Body

    1. Urban Subsistence Farming Solutions
    2. Large Scale Sustainable Farming Solutions
    3. Hungry Kitchen (fast & healthy food services)

  2. Healthy Mind

    1. Safe Space (affordable housing)
    2. Sharp Mind (effective fitness models)
    3. Social, Nature, and New

  3. Healthy Surroundings

    1. Clean Water (Water filtration systems, Waste water treatment systems)
    2. Clean Air (pollution and dust free)

Phase 2: Beyond Survival

Free the Time

Ammaha Thrive's goal is to make the best Education, Arts & Entertainment accessible to all.

Phase 3: Truth

Free the Knowledge

At Ammaha Axiom, we intend to develop a new way of organizing the data based on its strength, we call it ‘the strength of truth’, a measurable parameter to evaluate the validity, position and hierarchy of a given data with respect to the established and organized data.

‘Strength of truth’ is based on categorical and hierarchical knowledge systems like

  1. Absolute truth (proven mathematical systems)
  2. Observational truth (verified scientific systems)
  3. Perceptional truth (recorded and validated sentient observations)
